Overview

Problem

How might we design a cloud storage system that emphasizes project management and collaboration, allowing users to easily organize and prioritize what files they need to work on?

Solution

ideaLAB is a cloud storage platform that focuses on project management for your files. ideaLAB allows you to prioritize tasks, set deadlines, and easily organize and separate all of your content -- whether it’s for work or your personal projects.

01 Understand

Gather requirements Problem definition

A client approached us with the task of developing a new cloud storage application to compete with some big-name competitors in the space (Google Drive, Dropbox, Box, Evernote, Pinterest).
To meet business goals and objectives, the application needed to include the following features: • saving content you find on the web (links, images, videos, etc.)
• organizing content using things like categories, tags, groups, and/or folders
• creating content (notes, documents, maybe spreadsheets)
• uploading files (videos, images, PDFs, etc.) from a computer or mobile device

The client also wanted to explore collaboration features as well: • sharing a single item/groups of items with someone else (and vice-versa)
• connecting with other users for real-time collaboration in notes or documents
